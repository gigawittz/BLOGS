EID MUBARAK


May 25, 2020 by Gourav Sharma




India celebrating EID-ul-fitr amid COVID-19,The Shahi Imam of jama Masjid delhi saturday announced that EID-ul-fitr will be celebrated on May 25and appealed people to take precautions and maintain social distancing , he appealed to follow all the guidelines of government regarding COVID-19. EID is the one of the most significant festivals that is celebrated by the Muslim community .EID-ul-fitr marks the end of the holy month of Ramadan. As we all know India is a land known for its Unity in diversity and we all are so mixed with each other that every festival is celebrated with same enthusiasm, After the 2 months of lockdown markets are now partially opened everywhere and we know that the festivals help in boosting economy ,let us pray that this eid brings happiness and prosperity to the country and a better health to its people.
